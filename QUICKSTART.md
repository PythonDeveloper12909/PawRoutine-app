## Troubleshooting

### Tasks not showing?
- Make sure you selected the correct pet
- Check that the routine frequency is set to "Daily" or matches today's day of week

### Can't log in?
- Verify email and password are correct
- Passwords are case-sensitive

### Lost your data?
- Data is stored in your browser's localStorage
- Clearing browser data will delete your PawRoutine data
- Always export backups regularly!

### App not loading?
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection
